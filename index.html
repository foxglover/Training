Social Engineering Education

an experiment for self-employed people


Thank you for taking the time to test this educational tool for self-employed people.

You will be asked to complete three tasks (not necessarily in this order).

  • Complete a survey
  • Watch 4 short videos
  • Take a quick quiz to consolidate knowledge learnt from the videos

The whole process takes around 20 minutes in total. You can stop at any time.

All data collected will be anonymous and used in my MSc Computer Science research thesis for the University of Plymouth, discussing social engineering awareness in the self-employed community and the efficacy of this training package.

You can contact me at jennifer.sercombe@postgrad.plymouth.ac.uk with any questions.
